Check if your children can get free school meals

Here you can:

  • enter information to find out if your children can get free school meals
  • send the decision on to your children’s school, college, nursery or childcare setting

It should take about 5 minutes to complete.

Guidance

Read guidance about who can get free school meals by choosing from the following sections.

What you’ll need

While using this service, you will need:

  • to create or sign in to a OneGov account
  • a National Insurance number or asylum support reference number (previously NASS reference number)
  • to tell us details about any children you want to get free school meals, including the name of their school
