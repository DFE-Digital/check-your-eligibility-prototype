Your children are entitled to free school meals

What to do next

To get free school meals for your children, follow these steps online. This typically takes just a few minutes.

  1. Create or sign into your OneGov account.
  2. Enter the details of your children and their school.
  3. These will be sent on to the school, which will arrange free school meals.
