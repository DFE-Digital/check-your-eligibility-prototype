What to do

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You will need to complete a separate form for each school.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

You have access to a printer

  1. Download, print off and fill in the 'Free school meals claim form'.
  2. Give to the parent to fill in with their details along with any evidence they have that they are entitled.
  3. As a school administrator you can run a check for one parent or guardian to check their eligibility.

You do not have access to a printer

  1. Ask your LA to download the 'Free school meals claim form' and post it to the parent.
  2. Ask the parent to complete the paper form and return it to the school.
  3. Run the check as normal.
