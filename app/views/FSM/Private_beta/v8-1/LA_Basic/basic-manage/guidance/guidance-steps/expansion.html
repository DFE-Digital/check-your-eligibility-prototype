From the start of the 2026 to 2027 academic year, there will be 2 categories of eligibility for free school meals.

Targeted free school meals

Targeted

This covers children in households in receipt of Universal Credit with annual household earnings of £7,400 or less.

These children:

  • will get free school meals
  • will attract pupil premium and other disadvantage funding for their schools
  • may be eligible for additional support, such as the holiday activities and food programme (HAF) and the ‘extended rights’ element of school travel assistance

Expanded free school meals

Expanded

This covers children who are in households receiving Universal Credit with annual household earnings exceeding £7,400. Children will be entitled to free school meals only.

More information

For full details, read Free school meals guidance for schools and local authorities.
