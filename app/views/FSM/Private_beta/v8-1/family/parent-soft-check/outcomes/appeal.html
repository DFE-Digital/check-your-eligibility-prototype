Apply for free school meals with supporting evidence

You will need one of the following pieces of evidence:

  • up to three of your latest Universal Credit statements
  • a letter of Entitlement for Income Support
  • a letter of Entitlement for income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • a letter of Entitlement for the guaranteed element of Pension Credit
  • documents showing you are receiving relevant support under part VI of the Immigration and Asylum Act 1999

You can choose to apply online or in person.

Apply online

  1. Sign in or create a GOV.UK One Login.
  2. Add the details of all your children and their schools.
  3. Upload your evidence.
  4. Send your application to the school.

Apply in person

  1. complete the pdf form
  2. gather your evidence
  3. make an appointment to attend your children's school
  4. Your school or local authority will review your application and evidence
