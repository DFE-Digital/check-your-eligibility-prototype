Targeted and expanded free school meals

From the start of the 2026 to 2027 academic year, there will be 2 categories of eligibility for free school meals:

Targeted: this covers children in households in receipt of Universal Credit with annual household earnings of £7,400 or less. These children will attract pupil premium and other disadvantage funding for their schools.

Expanded: this covers children who are in households receiving Universal Credit with annual household earnings exceeding £7,400. Children will be entitled to free school meals only.
