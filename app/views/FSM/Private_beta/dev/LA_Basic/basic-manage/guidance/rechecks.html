This guidance explains how and when to carry out annual free school meal rechecks following the end of transitional protection.

Background

Transitional protection for free school meals ends after the 2025 to 2026 academic year. From summer 2026 onwards, you must complete annual rechecks to confirm ongoing eligibility.

When to recheck

You must:

  • complete annual rechecks between the last week of the spring term and the end of September in the autumn term
  • start rechecks no earlier than the final week of the spring term
  • finish all rechecks by the end of September

Applicants confirmed as eligible remain eligible for the full academic year.

Example

If you recheck an application in July 2026 and it is eligible, it remains eligible until August 2027. Complete the next recheck from the spring term in 2027.

How to complete a recheck

Use the batch check process to complete rechecks. Before you start, make sure you:

  • have up-to-date parent or guardian details
  • only include pupils you need to recheck
  • use the latest CSV template

To complete a batch check follow the guidance for running a batch check.

The batch check guidance includes an explanation of result types.

After completing rechecks

After running rechecks:

  • update your records with the latest eligibility status
  • ensure children are correctly recorded for funding and census returns
  • review any evidence for children with results of not eligible

Rechecking non-eligible results

If an application returns a result of 'not eligible', you should not run another recheck immediately afterwards. Recent changes from data sources may not yet be reflected, so repeating the check straight away is unlikely to change the outcome.

Only recheck again:

  • in the next term
  • after a known change in the family’s circumstances (for example, a new benefits claim) or the family has corrected the information held about them
  • if the family has requested a recheck

This approach improves accuracy and reduces unnecessary rechecks.

Help and support

For further help, contact the support team using the Customer help portal.
