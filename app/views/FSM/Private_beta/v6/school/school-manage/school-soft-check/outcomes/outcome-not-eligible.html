May not be eligible

The children of this parent may not be eligible for free school meals.

From the information you’ve provided, we cannot confirm if your children are eligible for free school meals.

If the parent wishes to appeal, you’ll need to send their supporting evidence to your local authority for review.

How to appeal

  1. Go to the guidance for reviewing evidence page to see a complete list of acceptable evidence types. If you haven’t received any evidence yet, contact the the parent or guardian.
  2. Select 'Appeal now' on this page and provide the information requested.
  3. Once completed, you will receive further instructions on how to send on supporting evidence to your local authority.
Complete list of acceptable evidence

To support your claim for free school meals, you can send:

  • up to three of your latest Universal Credit statements
  • your latest tax credit statement form TC602A (Award Notices) or TC603R (Auto Renewal), showing you are receiving Working Tax Credit run-on
  • your latest tax credit statement form TC602A (Award Notices) or TC603R (Auto Renewal), showing you are receiving Child Tax Credit
  • a letter of Entitlement for Income Support
  • a letter of Entitlement for income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • a letter of Entitlement for the guaranteed element of Pension Credit
  • documents showing you are receiving relevant support under part VI of the Immigration and Asylum Act 1999

Further guidance

More in-depth information on supporting evidence can be found in the guidance notes for parents and guardians.
