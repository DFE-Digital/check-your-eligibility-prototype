Take a photo or scan paper copies of your evidence to upload and send for review.

Before you upload it

It is important that your evidence can be read by our system or it will be rejected, so make sure:

  • all the text can be read easily
  • it is on 1 page with your contact details visibile
  • the benefit or amount you earn is visible
  • your National Insurance number is visible
  • the whole statement or letter is visible including all four corners

Take a photo

  1. Put the document on a flat surface, make sure it has no creases or folds and is lying straight.
  2. It should be well lit and without any shadows.
  3. The whole of the document should be visible in the photo.

Scan it

  1. Make sure the scanner is connected to a laptop or other device.
  2. Place the document text side down on the scanner. If your document is 2-sided, you must scan the side which shows your details.
  3. Make sure the document is lying straight and there are no creases or folds.
  4. Save the scanned image somewhere that's easy to find later.
