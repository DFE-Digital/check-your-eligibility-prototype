Children eligible

Your children are eligible for free school meals.

Apply for free school meals for your children

This usually takes a few minutes.

  1. Sign in or create a GOV.UK One Login.
  2. Add the details of all your children and their schools.
  3. Send your application to the school.
  4. We'll send an application confirmation email to the email address used on your GOV.UK One Login.
  5. Your children's school will arrange the free school meals.
