You can:

  • export and upload a CTF from your LA system in XML format

Extracting a Common Transfer File (CTF)

  1. Open your LA management information system (this could be SIMS or XXX or any other system your school uses) in the admin area of your LA management information system.
  2. Select export pupil data.
  3. Save the exported file on your computer.
  4. Add any information that your system doesn't collect (for example National Insurance numbers and pupil details).
  5. Save it as an XML file.

Uploading our CSV template

  1. Download the batch check template (185kb).
  2. Add the details of the {{ data['check-type'] }} you need to check as well as the children who will be added to the application.
  3. Make sure you include:
    • last name
    • date of birth (format DD/MM/YYYY or YYYY-MM-DD)
    • National Insurance number
    • Childs name
    • Childs date of birth (format DD/MM/YYYY or YYYY-MM-DD)
    • School the child attends on a full time basis
  4. Save it as a CSV file.
