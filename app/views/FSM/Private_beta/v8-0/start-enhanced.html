Manage eligibility for free school meals

Local authorities, schools and multi-academy trusts can use this service to manage and check eligibility for free school meals.

Sign in to:

  • run eligibility checks
  • download an eligibility form for parents
  • view guidance

Depending on your organisation's access, you may also be able to review applications, finalise applications, generate reports or search records.
