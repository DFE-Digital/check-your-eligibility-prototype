Use the navigation at the top to view Free school meals or Childcare eligibility prototypes to:

  • review end to end journeys that are currently being designed or tested with our researchers

  • Review the documentation that has been collated for the project.

How we design

We design this service using GDS and DfE design standards:

  • GDS Design Principles
  • GOV.UK Design System
  • DfE Design manual
  • DfE Design system

We have also used design patterns and components from DWP and MoJ.

Where we have explored different approaches we have fed these back into the relevant design system teams as well as the DfE Community and the #patterns-and-components Design ops team to ensure consistent use of patterns.

Content design

We use these content standards for designing content in the service:

  • GDS: Content design: planning, writing and managing content
  • GOV.UK style guide
  • Content design in DfE
  • DfE content style guide
