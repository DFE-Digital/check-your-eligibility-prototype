You are not eligible for free school meals

If you think this is wrong and you should be able to get free school meals for your child, you can make an appeal against the decision.

You can make an appeal by uploading a document showing you have 1 of the following:

  • Universal Credit number
  • National asylum support number
  • Jobseeker's Allowance support number
  • Universal credit
  • Income support
  • Income based jobseeker's allowance
  • Pension credit
  • Child Tax Credit
  • Working Tax Credit
