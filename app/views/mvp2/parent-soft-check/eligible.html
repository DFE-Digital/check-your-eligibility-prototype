Your children are entitled to free school meals

What to do next

To get free school meals for your children, follow these steps online. This usually takes a few minutes.

  1. Sign in or create a GOV.UK One Login account.
  2. Enter the details of all your children and their schools.
  3. Submit your application to send it to your school.
  4. We'll send you an email to the one you registered with to let you know it has been completed.
  5. Your children's school will arrange the free school meals.
